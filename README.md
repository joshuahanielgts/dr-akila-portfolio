# Dr. K. Akila - Academic Portfolio

This project is a modern, single-page academic portfolio website designed to showcase the professional profile, research, and achievements of **Dr. K. Akila**.

## Teacher Profile

**Dr. K. Akila, M.E., Ph.D.** is a highly experienced academic and researcher with nearly two decades of teaching experience (**19.7 years**). She currently serves as an **Assistant Professor (Selection Grade)** in the **Department of Computer Science and Engineering** at the **SRM Institute of Science and Technology, Vadapalani**.

### Key Highlights
- **Qualifications:** M.E., Ph.D. in Computer Science and Engineering.
- **Experience:** 19.7 years of dedicated teaching experience in higher education.
- **Research Areas:** Expertise in Image Processing, Human Action Recognition, Artificial Intelligence (AI), Machine Learning (ML), and Computer Vision.
- **Academic Contributions:** Authored **76** peer-reviewed publications and holds **5** patents.

This portfolio serves as a comprehensive digital presence, highlighting her academic journey, research contributions, and professional achievements in the field of Computer Science.

## Technology Stack
- Built with React and TypeScript.
- Styled with Tailwind CSS.
- Uses TanStack Router / TanStack Start for routing and framework features.
- Animations powered by Framer Motion.
